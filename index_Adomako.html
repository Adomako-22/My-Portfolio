Achievements

Recipient of the President’s International Scholarship of Excellence

After graduating as the best student among my peers in high school, I had dreams of continuing my education—but reality hit hard. Raised by a single parent and with no financial support, I spent two long years at home, uncertain of what the future held. Despite my academic achievements, I feared I might end up on the streets with no path forward.

But I never gave up. I shared my story on LinkedIn, hoping someone would see the potential in me—and then, everything changed. I was awarded the President’s International Scholarship of Excellence to study at York University. That opportunity didn’t just open a door for me—it changed my life.

Now, I’m building my future at York, determined to use this second chance to become a financial and data analyst, and give back to communities like the one that raised me.
